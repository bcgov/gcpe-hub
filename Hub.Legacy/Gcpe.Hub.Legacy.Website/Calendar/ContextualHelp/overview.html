Help for Overview Section

Not For Look Ahead

Select if this item is highly confidential or sensitive. It’s advisable to include **STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL** in title field for added emphasis.

Summary

Describe key details. For instance, list who, how much and for what with funding announcement entries. Examples: MEDIA AVAIL & NR: Minister XXX makes funding announcement to support XYZ with a one-time grant of $####. NR ONLY: One-time grant announced for XYZ.

Issue

If this item is a current or potential media issue, or an issue for government in any way based on topic, this box must be clicked.

Significance

How will this impact people? What difference will it make? Why is it important?
