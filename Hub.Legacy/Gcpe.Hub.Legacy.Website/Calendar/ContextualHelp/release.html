Help for Release Section

This section must be completed whenever a release is being issued. Fill out the Origin field whether it is a ministry release or third-party.

Translations Required:

This field must be filled out if translations are planned for ministry releases. This info is tracked by others.

Themes

For ministry releases only. Select relevant theme(s). This pulls the release into related news feeds on gov.bc.ca. Use the following guide:

Birth, Adoption, Death, Marriage, and Divorce
e.g. Births, marriages, deaths; legal changes of name; adoptions; order certificates, copies or extracts; online birth registration; separation & divorce; will notices.

British Columbians and Our Governments
e.g. The ministries, about our government; open government; DataBC; govTogetherBC; news; celebrating B.C.; BC Stats; First Nation treaties; Indigenous peoples; Services for Government; government identification; freedom of information; local governments.

Driving and Transportation
e.g. Licensing & insurance; DriveBC; RoadSafetyBC; passenger air rail & water; highway construction & maintenance; B.C. Ferries.

Education and Training
e.g. Early learning; K-12, post secondary; adult learning; ways to learn; K to 12 administration; skills training; StudentAidBC; Indigenous Education; BC for High School; Enhanced District Reporting.

Employment, Business and Economic Development
e.g. Jobs in B.C.; small business; not-for-profit societies; cooperatives; employment standards; Employers’ Advisers Office; international trade; attracting investment; B.C. exports; economic development; funding and grants.

Environmental Protection and Sustainability
e.g. Climate change; waste management; air, land & water, plants & wildlife; environmental emergencies; pesticides & pest management; and Conservation Officer Services.

Family and Social Supports
e.g. Supports for seniors; parents & youth; income assistance; disability assistance; foster parenting; accessibility; and childcare.

Farming, Natural Resources and Industry
e.g. Agriculture & seafood; B.C. building code, electricity; forestry; highway construction; liquefied natural gas, mineral exploration & mining; natural gas & oil; and Site C dam.

Health
e.g. MSP; PharmaCare; accessing health care; public health; health forms; practitioner & professional resources; Office of the Provincial Health Officer; and mental health and substance use supports in B.C.

Housing and Tenancy
e.g. Rental housing; strata housing; seniors housing; owning a home; social housing; buying & selling property; building or renovating; and B.C. building code.

Law, Crime and Justice
e.g. Response to COVID-19; B.C. prosecution service; Corrections; courthouse services; criminal justice; human rights; Justice Access Centres; justice reform; policing; sex offender registry; victim services; and VictimLinkBC.

Public Safety and Emergency Services
e.g. Criminal record checks; domestic violence; emergency management; fire safety; Helpline for Children; RoadSafetyBC; PreparedBC; protecting children; Protection Order Registry; security industry licensing; missing persons.

Sports, Recreation, Arts and Culture
e.g. Amateur sports; B.C. Games; B.C. Parks; recreation; arts; culture; libraries; historic places; museums; funding; gaming grants and event hosting.

Taxes and Tax Credits
e.g. PST; home owner grant, first time home buyers, property transfers; carbon tax, tobacco tax, natural resource taxes, insurance tax and personal income tax.

Tourism and Immigration
e.g. Traveling to B.C., studying in B.C.; working in B.C.; immigrating to B.C. and HelloBC.

News Subscribe:

For ministry releases only. Select if related to BC Coroners Service, CleanBC, COVID-19, Gender Equity and Housing Affordability. This sends the release directly to subscribers.
