Help for Schedule Section

Indicate when this item will (or may) occur. For TBC placeholders, the start of any date range should be the earliest possible date it ‘might’ happen. Refresh or update regularly until confirmed.

Start and End Times

Leave the 8am-6pm default settings as is until an exact start time is known (confirmed or tentative). For “All Day” items, click both the All Day box AND the Date Confirmed box.

Scheduling Considerations and Approval Notes

Where applicable, explain details and status ie. who has approved something to proceed or to be issued; what time frame is being requested; what may be holding it up, and so on. Be candid. Examples:

  • MO approved to issue; needs content sign-off. Date/time confirmed with CC.
  • MO & HQ Events approved to proceed on this date and time.
  • MO aiming for late fall; waiting on TB approval.
  • Date/time confirmed by third-party organizers.
